The Top Movies/Series In 2023 According To Me

1.Oppenheimer

"Oppenheimer" delves into philosophical themes surrounding the use of the dark side of technology, the nature of war, and the responsibilities of scientists towards society.

2.loki

Loki is a well-crafted series that is both entertaining and thought-provoking. It is a must-watch for any Marvel fan who wants to experience a fresh take on the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

3.Spider-man: across the spider-verse

The film has been praised for its spectacular animation, which brings the comic book world of Spider-Man to life in a way that has never been done before. The filmmakers have managed to strike a perfect balance of humanity with dazzling visuals, allowing the story to be one of the more satisfying Spider-Man stories ever told
